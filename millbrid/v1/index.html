MillionBridges, a Barcelona based fintech partners with Canadian financial services company RevoluPay, to bring to market a unique web-based tool that will help Lebanese living abroad channel their remittances to their family back home, in a safe and secure manner.

MillionBridges will launch its service in November 2020. If you would like to stay informed about our services, please contact us to stay tuned!
